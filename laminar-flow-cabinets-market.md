<p><p>Laminar flow cabinets are crucial in providing a sterile work environment in laboratories and cleanrooms. Horizontal laminar flow cabinets direct airflow from the back to the front, offering a clear view and easy access to equipment, ideal for medium-sized tasks. In contrast, vertical laminar flow cabinets draw air from the top, providing additional protection for sensitive samples and users, making them suitable for handling hazardous materials. Both types ensure minimal contamination, catering to distinct operational needs in various scientific and medical applications.</p></p>
