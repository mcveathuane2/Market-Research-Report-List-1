<p><p>The automotive biosensors market encompasses various types, including thermal and piezo-electric biosensors. Thermal biosensors are used to monitor temperature changes in engine components and passenger comfort, ensuring optimal performance and safety. Piezo-electric biosensors detect pressure or mechanical stress, enabling the measurement of various parameters like tire pressure and cabin vibrations. Both types enhance vehicle efficiency, safety, and user experience by providing real-time data for better decision-making and system responses in modern automotive applications.</p></p>
