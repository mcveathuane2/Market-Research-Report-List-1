<p><p>The square lithium battery market comprises various components critical for performance and efficiency. Cathode materials, such as lithium iron phosphate (LFP) and lithium nickel manganese cobalt oxide (NMC), determine energy density and stability. Anode materials, mainly graphite and silicon composites, impact charge capacity and cycle life. Diaphragms, usually made from porous materials, ensure electrolyte flow while preventing short circuits. Nonaqueous electrolytes, featuring organic solvents and lithium salts, enable high ionic conductivity and overall battery performance, essential for various applications.</p></p>
