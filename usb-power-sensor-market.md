<p><p>The USB power sensor market is categorized into several types based on measurement capabilities. Average Power/CW sensors measure continuous power levels, ideal for ongoing signal analysis. Peak Power sensors capture maximum power levels during signal bursts, useful for short-duration events. Pulse Power sensors analyze power in pulse modulated signals, measuring transient characteristics. Other types may include specialized sensors tailored for unique applications. Each type serves different industries, ensuring precise power measurements for various electronic devices and systems.</p></p>
