<p><ul><li>Mechanics Pump</li><li>Electronic Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The ambulatory infusion pump market consists of two primary types: mechanical pumps and electronic pumps. Mechanical pumps are gravity-driven or peristaltic devices that rely on manual adjustments to control flow rates, making them straightforward and low-maintenance. In contrast, electronic pumps utilize advanced technology, allowing for programmable settings, automated flow control, and data tracking. These electronic models often enhance patient comfort and safety through precise medication delivery and monitoring features, catering to a broad spectrum of healthcare needs.</p></p>
