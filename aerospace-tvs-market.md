<p><strong>The Aerospace TVS Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Uni-polar TVS</li><li>Bi-polar TVS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Aerospace TVS market is categorized into uni-polar and bi-polar transient voltage suppressors. Uni-polar TVS devices are designed to protect circuits against positive voltage spikes, making them ideal for applications where only positive voltage is present. In contrast, bi-polar TVS devices can handle both positive and negative voltage transients, providing versatile protection in various aerospace electronics. These distinctions are crucial for ensuring the reliability and longevity of electronic systems in the demanding aerospace environment.</p></p>
