<p><p>Textile printing auxiliary market types encompass various chemical agents that enhance the printing process. Dispersants improve dye distribution, ensuring even application. Crosslinking agents enhance adhesion between dyes and fabrics, improving durability. Softeners enhance the tactile quality of textiles. Emulsifiers help mix water-based and oil-based substances, ensuring uniformity. Defoamers reduce surface tension, minimizing foam during application. Thickeners adjust viscosity for better control during printing, while adhesives provide bonding strength for printed designs. Other auxiliaries may also include various specialized agents needed in specific printing processes.</p></p>
