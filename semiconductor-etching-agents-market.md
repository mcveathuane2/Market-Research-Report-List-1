<p><p>The semiconductor etching agents market is divided into two main types: wet etching agents and dry etching agents. Wet etching agents utilize liquid chemicals to remove material from semiconductor surfaces, providing uniformity and high selectivity. In contrast, dry etching agents employ plasma or gaseous chemicals, allowing for greater precision and the ability to create intricate patterns on substrates. Both types are essential in semiconductor fabrication, catering to different processing needs and contributing to advancements in microelectronics.</p></p>
