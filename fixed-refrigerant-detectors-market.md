<p><strong>The Fixed Refrigerant Detectors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Halide</li><li>Electronic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fixed refrigerant detectors market includes two primary types: halide and electronic detectors. Halide detectors utilize a chemical reaction to identify the presence of refrigerants, often emitting a distinctive color change. They are typically easy to use but may require recalibration or replacement. In contrast, electronic detectors employ advanced sensors to detect refrigerant leaks with greater accuracy and reliability. They often feature digital displays and can monitor multiple refrigerants, making them ideal for more complex applications in HVAC systems.</p></p>
