<p><p>The silicone fiberglass sleeving market is divided into two main types: braided and non-braided. Braided silicone fiberglass sleeving features a woven structure, providing enhanced strength, flexibility, and abrasion resistance, making it ideal for applications that require high durability. Non-braided silicone fiberglass sleeving, on the other hand, is typically smoother and offers good thermal insulation, making it suitable for applications where heat resistance is critical but physical stress is lower. Both types cater to various industrial and electrical insulation needs.</p></p>
