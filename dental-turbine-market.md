<p><strong>The Dental Turbine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Inside Exhaust</li><li>Outside Exhaust</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The dental turbine market is categorized into two main types based on exhaust design: inside exhaust and outside exhaust. Inside exhaust turbines direct the air and debris generated during use back through the handpiece, providing a cleaner working environment within the dental cavity. In contrast, outside exhaust turbines expel air and particles away from the patient, enhancing visibility and hygiene during procedures. Both types offer distinct advantages, catering to different preferences and operational needs in dental practices.</p></p>
