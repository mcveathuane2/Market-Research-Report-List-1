<p><p>The haul truck tire market features three primary types: pneumatic, solid, and polyurethane tires. Pneumatic tires are air-filled, providing excellent shock absorption and traction, designed for varying terrains. Solid tires are made from resilient materials, offering durability and puncture resistance, ideal for harsh environments. Polyurethane tires combine the benefits of both, providing a lightweight, flexible option with enhanced wear resistance and reduced maintenance. Each type serves specific operational needs, balancing performance, longevity, and cost-effectiveness in haul truck applications.</p></p>
