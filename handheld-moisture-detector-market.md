<p><p>The handheld moisture detector market includes three primary types: resistive, capacitive, and others. </p><p>Resistive moisture detectors measure moisture content by evaluating the electrical resistance between two probes, providing quick and simple results. Capacitive detectors function by assessing changes in capacitance caused by moisture levels, offering non-invasive measurement and greater sensitivity. The "others" category encompasses various emerging technologies and specialized devices, catering to niche applications and unique user needs, thus expanding the overall market landscape.</p></p>
