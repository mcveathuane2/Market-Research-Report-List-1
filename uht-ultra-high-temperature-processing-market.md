<p><p>UHT (Ultra-High Temperature) processing involves heating liquids to extremely high temperatures for a short time to destroy pathogens and extend shelf life. The market can be categorized into direct and indirect types. Direct UHT processing injects steam into the liquid, rapidly heating it, while indirect processing heats the liquid through contact with heated surfaces. Both methods are crucial in the dairy, juice, and liquid food industries, ensuring products remain safe and stable without refrigeration for extended periods.</p></p>
