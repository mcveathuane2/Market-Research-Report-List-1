<p><strong>The Cell Culture Protein Surface Coatings Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Self-coating</li><li>Pre-coating</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cell culture protein surface coatings market is categorized into self-coating and pre-coating technologies. Self-coating involves surfaces that naturally adsorb proteins from the culture medium, promoting cell attachment and growth without prior treatment. In contrast, pre-coating refers to surfaces that are deliberately treated with specific proteins or polymers before cell culture, enhancing the predictability and consistency of cell behavior. Both methods aim to optimize cell proliferation and functionality, supporting advancements in biotechnology and regenerative medicine.</p></p>
