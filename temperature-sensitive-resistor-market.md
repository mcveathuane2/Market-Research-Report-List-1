<p><ul><li>NTC Type</li><li>PTC Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The temperature-sensitive resistor market consists mainly of two types: NTC (Negative Temperature Coefficient) and PTC (Positive Temperature Coefficient) resistors. NTC resistors decrease resistance as temperature rises, making them ideal for applications like temperature sensing and compensation. In contrast, PTC resistors increase resistance with rising temperature, which is useful for overcurrent protection and self-regulating heating applications. Both types cater to diverse industries, including automotive, consumer electronics, and industrial automation, driven by the need for precise temperature control and monitoring.</p></p>
