<p><ul><li>Full Container Load (FCL)</li><li>Less-than Container Load (LCL)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Land Freight Forwarding market comprises two main types: Full Container Load (FCL) and Less-than Container Load (LCL). FCL involves transporting goods in an entire container, making it ideal for large shipments, ensuring direct delivery with reduced handling risks. Conversely, LCL caters to smaller shipments, consolidating cargo from multiple clients into a single container. This option is economical for those with limited volume while maximizing space efficiency. Both methods offer distinct advantages based on shipment size and cost considerations.</p></p>
