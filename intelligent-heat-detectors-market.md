<p><p>Intelligent heat detectors are essential for fire safety, categorized mainly into fixed temperature and differential temperature detectors. Fixed temperature detectors activate an alarm when a specific temperature threshold is reached, ensuring immediate response to fire hazards. In contrast, differential temperature detectors monitor changes in ambient temperature; they trigger an alert if the temperature rise exceeds a predetermined rate, allowing for earlier detection of potential fires. Both types enhance safety in various environments by offering reliable monitoring of thermal conditions.</p></p>
