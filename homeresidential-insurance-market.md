<p><strong>The Home/Residential Insurance Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>

<p><p>The home/residential insurance market comprises various types of coverage tailored to specific needs. Homeowners insurance protects structures and personal property from risks like fire or theft, covering both the dwelling and liability. Contents insurance focuses solely on the protection of personal belongings within a home, often beneficial for renters or homeowners without structural coverage. Renters insurance provides liability and personal property protection for individuals leasing a property, ensuring their belongings are safeguarded against unforeseen events without covering the building itself.</p></p>
