<p><p>The hydraulic steering systems market comprises primarily two types: mechanical and electronic. In contrast, electronic hydraulic steering systems leverage electronic controls to optimize responsiveness and efficiency, enabling features like active feedback and advanced driver assistance systems. Both types cater to different automotive needs, with mechanical systems favored for their durability and cost-effectiveness, while electronic systems are increasingly popular for their enhanced functionality and adaptability.</p></p>
