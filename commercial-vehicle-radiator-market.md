<p><p>The commercial vehicle radiator market features primarily two types: aluminum radiators and copper radiators. Aluminum radiators are favored for their lightweight, corrosion resistance, and effective heat transfer, making them ideal for modern vehicles. In contrast, copper radiators, known for superior thermal conductivity and durability, are often used in heavy-duty applications, providing robustness and reliability. The choice between these materials depends on factors like vehicle type, cost efficiency, and specific cooling requirements, shaping the dynamics of the market.</p></p>
