<p><p>The ferrosilicon alloy materials market primarily consists of two types: milled ferrosilicon products and atomized ferrosilicon products. Milled ferrosilicon is produced through grinding ferrosilicon into fine powder, making it suitable for various applications, including foundries and steel production. Atomized ferrosilicon, on the other hand, is created by rapidly cooling molten ferrosilicon, resulting in fine particles with high purity. These products are essential in metallurgy and manufacturing, serving to enhance metal properties and improve alloy performance.</p></p>
