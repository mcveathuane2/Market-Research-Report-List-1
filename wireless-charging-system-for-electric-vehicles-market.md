<p><ul><li>Electromagnetic Induction</li><li>Magnetic Resonance</li><li>Magneto-Dynamic Coupling</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The wireless charging system for electric vehicles (EVs) employs three main technologies: electromagnetic induction, magnetic resonance, and magneto-dynamic coupling. Electromagnetic induction uses magnetic fields to transfer energy between coils placed close together, making it efficient for stationary charging. Magnetic resonance enables energy transfer over slightly greater distances by allowing resonant coupling between transmitter and receiver coils. Magneto-dynamic coupling utilizes fluctuating magnetic fields to transmit energy, offering flexibility in alignment and positioning. Each technology provides unique benefits in convenience and efficiency for EV charging.</p></p>
