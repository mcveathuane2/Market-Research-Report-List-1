<p><p>Circuit breakers and fuses are essential components in electrical systems, providing protection against overcurrent and short circuits. A circuit breaker is an automatic switch that interrupts the flow of electricity when it detects a fault, allowing for resets and reuse. In contrast, fuses consist of a metal wire that melts when excessive current flows, breaking the circuit, and requiring replacement.</p><p>The Circuit Breaker and Fuse Market is poised for significant growth, projected to expand at a CAGR of 5.8% during the forecast period.
