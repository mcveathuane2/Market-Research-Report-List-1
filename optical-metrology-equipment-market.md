<p><strong>The Optical Metrology Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Contact Equipment</li><li>Contactless Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The optical metrology equipment market consists of two primary types: contact and contactless equipment. Contact equipment physically interacts with the object being measured, ensuring high precision through direct surface contact, making it ideal for small-scale applications. Conversely, contactless equipment utilizes light-based techniques, such as lasers and cameras, to measure objects without physical contact. This allows for measurements of larger or delicate items while minimizing risk of damage, making it suitable for diverse industrial applications and environments.</p></p>
