<p><p>The cold packs market consists of two main types: repeatable packs and disposable packs. Repeatable packs are designed for multiple uses, often made from durable materials that can be refrozen after use, offering long-term cost efficiency and convenience. In contrast, disposable packs are single-use products, typically pre-filled with a cooling gel or liquid that activates upon activation, providing immediate relief. Both types cater to varying consumer needs, with repeatable packs appealing to frequent users and disposable packs favored for their convenience and instant access.</p></p>
