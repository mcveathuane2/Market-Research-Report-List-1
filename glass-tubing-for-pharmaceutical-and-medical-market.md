<p><p>Glass tubing in the pharmaceutical and medical markets is primarily categorized into two types: soda lime glass and borosilicate glass. Soda lime glass is cost-effective and widely used for applications like vials and containers, but it has lower thermal resistance. In contrast, borosilicate glass offers superior thermal stability and chemical resistance, making it ideal for high-precision applications such as lab equipment and pharmaceutical storage. Both types are integral to ensuring safety and efficacy in medical and pharmaceutical products.</p></p>
